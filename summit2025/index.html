Transparency.dev Summit

Join us for the Transparency.dev Summit in Gothenburg, Sweden, on October 20-22, 2025.

This event aims to bring together implementers, operators, and clients of real world transparency systems in order to meet peers, share best practices, and learn about the latest developments in the community.

In addition to a variety of transparency-log topics, the event will feature dedicated sessions for the Certificate Transparency community (e.g., a colocated CT day style event).
