Transparency.dev Summit
Code of Conduct

All event participants, whether attending in-person or participating virtually, are expected to behave in accordance with professional standards, venue policies and applicable laws, and the Transparency.dev Community Code of Conduct. We expect cooperation from all participants to help ensure a safe and respectful environment for everyone.

To report an incident, please contact a conference organizer on site, or email transparency-community-admin@googlegroups.com or Transparency.dev Community Manager, Kat Joyce (kat@taknira.com).
