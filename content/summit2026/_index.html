Transparency.dev Summit 2026

Join us for the 2026 Transparency.dev Summit in Montreal, Canada!

This event aims to bring together implementers, operators, and clients of real world transparency systems in order to meet peers, share best practices, and learn about the latest developments in the community.

Accommodation

There are many accommodation options near the Google Montreal offices. For guidance, the DoubleTree by Hilton Montreal is a decent mid-tier hotel, and both Humaniti and Hôtel Monville are more luxurious options. However, there are also many other hotel and hostel choices available in the surrounding area that are worth exploring if these do not meet your needs.
